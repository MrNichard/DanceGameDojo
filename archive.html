ITL 2023 begins!

The International Timing League, a yearly online ITG tech tournament, has finally started as of March 18th, 2023. The event is organized by the International Timing Collective (ITC) and features charts from members in the community. Chart difficulties start as low as ITG 7s, so whether you're an expert or just starting out there should be something here for everybody. For more information regarding the tournament & how to join, please refer to the following links.

  • ITC Discord
  • @IntlTiming Twitter
  • ITC.dance
  • ITL 2023

    • ECS 11.5 is here!

    March 1st marked the start of the annual team stamina, ECS 11.5. The event will run from March 1st, 2023, until the final day of submissions on May 14th, 2023. Even if you're not interested in competing, the ECS .5 events also act as a showcase of new stamina content, so be sure to check it out! Format information, downloads, acknowledgments, and other info can be found on the website for the event.
