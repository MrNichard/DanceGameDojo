A bit about the site

Since June of 2020 I have been an avid Dance Game enthusiast, and I find myself constantly looking up more and more info on the games I play. I decided to make a hotspot for for all information regarding dance games.

Dance Games are one of my biggest passions, and the same can be said for a lot of other people. My goal is to create a sight where players of all ages and skill levels can come together. Whether your a tech, stamina, mod chart, or casual player, there is going to be something here for everyone.

My goal for this site is to provide the player base with as much information as possible. This includes information on the games history, the various type/versions of those game, on there playstyle, and even give some useful tips to help new players improve.
