A bit about the site

Since June of 2020 I have been an avid Dance Game enthusiast, and I find myself constantly looking up more and more info on the games I play. I decided to make a hotspot for for all information regarding dance games.

Dance Games are one of my biggest passions, and the same can be said for a lot of other people. My goal is to create a sight where players of all ages and skill levels can come together. Whether your a tech, stamina, mod chart, or casual player, there is going to be something here for everyone.

My goal for this site is to provide the player base with as much information as possible. This includes information on the games history, the various type/versions of those game, on there playstyle, and even give some useful tips to help new players improve.

Other useful websites

ITGMANIA

ITGMANIA is a fork Stepmania 5.1-b2, made with the community in mind. Created by Natano and teejusb, this version contains such features as a build in Groovestats networking, and simply love (a popular SM/ITG theme) pre installed.

Groovestats

Created all the way back in 2004, Groovestats allows you to enter and track your scores for ITG & the Pump It Up Pro series. the sight allows you to set rivals, see the amount of quads (100%'s) you've gotten, and more.

PIUgame

PIUgame is Andamiros official website for there Pump It Up Series of dance games. Like Konamis eagate, it allows you track and view your scores. PIUgame also allows you to create and upload your own stepcharts for the community to download and play.

eagate.573

Eagate is Konamis official website, containing DDR as well as all there other rhythm games and various other games. It allows to see your ranking, change your settings, and see your play data.

eagle.ac

Eagle.ac is essentially the eagate for the rest of the world. Like eagate, is tracks and display your scores from DDR A20+. Howver unlike eagate, this version is used to track scores on cabinets that aren't connected to Konami's offcial network.
