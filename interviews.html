An Interview with Archi

Who is Archi: Archi (aka Archi Veflax) is a stamina player hailing from Pennsylvania, and is regarded by many as the godfather of ITG Stamina. Hes the creator of the Disbalanced series of simfile packs, and is the creator of the East Coast Stamina series of tournaments.
