A new tournament for new players

Recently the Pennsylvania DDR community made an annoucement that there will be a tournament where Expert charts will be excluded. As the name may suggest, this tournament is aimed more towards casuals & beginner players. The player cap is currently 20 players, all of relatively intermediate difficulty. The tournament is to be held at at Round1 Bowling & Amusement in Erie, Pennsylvania on July 29th.

    • DDR Storm is back baby!

    After nearly 4 whole years, ceogaming has annouced that DDR Storm will be making its grand return on June 23. DDR Storm (now Project Storm), is to be held at the Hilton Daytona Beach Oceanfront Resort in Daytona Florida from June 23rd-25th. The games to be featured this year will be DDR A20 Plus, Pump It Up XX, In The Groove 2 (Stepmania), & even StepmaniaX. For future updates regarding the tournament, please visit ceogamings website.

    • Eclipse East 2023 has been announced.

    The second itteration of the Maryland based dance game tournament, Eclipse East, has been confirmed to be happening this year. The tournament is set to be held at the Baltimore Marriot at Camden Yards in Baltimore Maryland, and will take place between December 8th thru 10th. The categories players can enter in are DDR A20, PIU XX, StepmaniaX, and Dancerush. For more info & update, please check out the link below.

    • ITL 2023 begins!

    The International Timing League, a yearly online ITG tech tournament, has finally started as of March 18th, 2023. The event is organized by the International Timing Collective (ITC) and features charts from members in the community. Chart difficulties start as low as ITG 7s, so whether you're an expert or just starting out there should be something here for everybody. For more information regarding the tournament, please refer to the following links.
