Welcome to Tips & Tricks

Here I'll provide some tips and tricks that can help both new player and old players improve in there craft.

Beginner Tips

If you are just starting out, these tips may be useful for you.

  • Use your heal to hit the down arrow
  • NEVER return to the middle unless game prompts you too (i.e with mines or shock arrows).
  • Try and find your max, and then work up from there
  • Pace yourself, don't push yourself too much.
  • Don't take it too seriously.
  • Make sure to enjoy yourself, don't get discouraged.

    • Tech Tips

    If you already in a decent spot with the game, these tips will help you with achieve more scores on a wider arrange of charts.

    VincentITG made a great video talking about Tech Primers, check it out here.

  • Start with lower level/bpm tech, and work up from that.
  • Try to break out of your comfort zone and push yourself.
  • If you want to, focus on one tech notation at a time.
  • Keep practicing different charts from different packs.
  • Just keep playing!

    • Stamina Tips

    If you already in a decent spot with the game, these tips will help you with achieve more scores on a wider arrange of charts.

    If you'd like to learn more about stamina, please check out these videos by Dom_ITG

  • Work on form, try and keep movement to a minimum
  • Find a bpm and level your comfortable streaming at, the go from there
  • Use baby powder/furniture polish if needed (helps aliviate friction)
  • Play songs from a wide variety of packs and stepartists.
  • Be sure to play bpms that are challenging to you.
  • Don't get discouraged.
